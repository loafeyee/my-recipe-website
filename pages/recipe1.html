Some amazing Recipe

A traditional dish prepared in the distinctive Yor style. Although some people believe that hunger is better to eating this food, this substantial, filling supper can endure for several months without going bad.

Ingredients

  • 1 Fresh Fish
  • 2 Whole Chickens
  • 7 Cups of Hopes and dreams
  • 2 Lemons
  • Salt to Taste

Directions

Step 1

Place the raw fish and chicken into a pot.

Step 2

Mash it together until it's slightly chunky.

Step 3

Grate two entire lemons and put it in the pot, set it on high.

Step 4

Pray to God.

Step 5

Compact it until firm, shape into a ball or any shape you so desire.

Step 6

Serve while "Fresh".
